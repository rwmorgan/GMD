Assessment Overview

This course delivers three TASC qualifications. All assessment is criterion-based — no exams, no surprise hurdles. Here's exactly how it works.

How criterion-based assessment works: Each criterion has a clearly defined standard. Your teacher rates your evidence as C (Satisfactory Standard), A (High Standard — ICT only), t (some achievement below C standard), or z (no evidence). Your final award is determined by the combination of ratings across all criteria. You'll see every standard before you start each task.
