Academic Integrity

All work submitted for TASC assessment must be your own. Using AI to write your research reports, reflections, or code without disclosure is a breach of academic integrity.

What is allowed:

  • Using free assets with correct attribution (Tasks 3.3, 3.4)
  • Following tutorials and adapting the code for your game (cite the tutorial)
  • Getting help from classmates on a concept (but writing your own response)

What is not allowed:

  • Submitting another student's work as your own
  • Copying text from the internet without attribution
  • Having AI write your reflections or research reports without disclosure
