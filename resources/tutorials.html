How to learn from a tutorial (and stay TASC-legal): ① Build the tutorial exactly as shown. ② Close the video and rebuild the mechanic from memory in your own project. ③ Change it — new variables, new behaviour, your assets. ④ Record the tutorial's title, author and URL in your GDD reference list. Steps ②–④ are what turn "copying" into evidence of learning for ICT C4 and PRJ C3.

Not Sure Which Engine?

Choose GameMaker if…

You're new to coding, your game is 2D, and you want maximum progress in minimum time. The lowest-risk choice for the 12-week project.

Choose Unity if…

You want to learn real C# programming, might go 3D, and care about industry-relevant skills on your résumé.

Choose Unreal if…

You want stunning 3D visuals without code (Blueprints), you've scoped tightly, and your machine can handle it.
