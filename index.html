How Assessment Works

This course uses criterion-based assessment — no exams. Your work is judged against clearly defined criteria from your TASC course documents.

You'll see exactly what a C (Satisfactory) and A (High Standard) look like for every task before you start it. No surprises.

Submissions go to your teacher via Canvas. Every task page on this site has a direct Canvas submission link.
